About Me

Engineering Meets
Curiosity & Precision

I am an Electrical Engineering graduate from the University of Indonesia, currently in an accelerated Bachelor–Master program with a specialisation in Electronic and Intelligent Embedded Systems. My work sits at the intersection of digital hardware and applied physics — from designing FPGA control architectures to characterising photonic sensors in the lab.

At BRIN, I developed an FBG-based hydrostatic pressure sensor validated through both finite element modelling and physical experiment — work that led to a Springer LNEE publication presented in Japan. At PLN Indonesia Power, I led a five-person team deploying Analog Signature Analysis for live PCB diagnostics in an industrial power environment.

Beyond engineering, I have led 11-member financial audit commissions, mentored incoming students, and coordinated large cross-functional teams — bringing structured thinking and ownership into every role I take on.
